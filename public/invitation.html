20th Annual Durga Puja 2018, Helsinki


Dear friends,

The arrival of autumn tells us that Durga Puja will soon be on its way here and brings to mind the cherished sounds of the dhak and conch. We are pleased to invite you to this auspicious occasion, which marks the victory of Good over Evil. This year we, the Bengalis in Finland, will be celebrating this festival of joy with our family and friends from 19th October to 21st October 2018. In this event, you will be able to experience our rich culture and customs, as colourful as the Finnish autumn.

PROGRAMME:
